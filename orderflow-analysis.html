Orderflow Analysis

Orderflow analysis reads the live supply-and-demand battle inside the orderbook, exposing real market intent.

Applications:

  • Identifying whale accumulation/distribution
  • Tracking liquidity walls
  • Detecting spoofing and manipulative behaviors
  • Volume delta & imbalance models
  • Orderbook heatmaps

Orderflow gives a microscopic view of how price truly moves beneath the candles.
