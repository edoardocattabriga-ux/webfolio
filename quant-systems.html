Quant Systems

Quantitative systems use mathematical models and statistical engines to generate trading signals. They remove emotion, rely on real data, and follow rules with machine precision.

Key traits:

  • Statistical arbitrage models
  • Mean-reversion & momentum engines
  • Probabilistic signal generation
  • Backtesting with large datasets
  • Execution automation with low-latency logic

Quant systems allow strategies that outperform manual trading through precise math and real-time adaptation.
