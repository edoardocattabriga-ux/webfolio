High-Frequency Trading Engines

HFT engines execute trades in microseconds, taking advantage of tiny inefficiencies before the market corrects.

Capabilities:

  • Microsecond-level execution
  • Ultra-low-latency order routing
  • Market-making bots
  • Arbitrage detection across exchanges
  • Fast statistical models

HFT engines require efficient infrastructure, optimized code, and real-time orderbook scanning.
